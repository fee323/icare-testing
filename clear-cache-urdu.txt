=== Browser Cache Clear Karne Ka Tareeqa ===

SABSE AASAN TAREEQA:

1. Chrome/Edge Browser Band Karo (completely close)
2. Phir se kholo aur ye steps follow karo:

   a) F12 press karo (DevTools khulega)
   b) Application tab pe jao (upar menu mein)
   c) Left side mein "Service Workers" pe click karo
   d) "Unregister" button dabao
   e) Left side mein "Storage" pe click karo
   f) "Clear site data" button dabao
   g) DevTools band karo
   h) Ctrl + Shift + Delete dabao
   i) "All time" select karo
   j) Sab checkboxes tick karo
   k) "Clear data" dabao
   l) Browser completely band karo
   m) Phir se kholo aur website kholo

YA PHIR:

Incognito/Private Window mein kholo:
- Ctrl + Shift + N (Chrome)
- Ctrl + Shift + P (Firefox)
- Phir website kholo: https://icare-diix1jkcv-wajahatfrontdev-8765s-projects.vercel.app
